# Atlas-School-PD1100-Week3-Assignment-OnlinePortfolio
Custom Online Portfolio

Requirements

About Me Section
Photo: A professional, well-lit photo. Use the same image across LinkedIn and your online portfolio to create a strong personal brand.
Paragraph: Write a short paragraph describing yourself, your skills, and your professional focus. Include links to companies or projects mentioned.

Portfolio / Projects Section
Showcase at least three projects:

Screenshot of each project
Short description of each project
Link to the GitHub README.md
Link to the deployed project (if available)

Resume
Include a copy of your draft resume for now (Week 2).
This can be updated in Week 5 with your final resume version.
Ensure it represents who you are and the type of roles you’re seeking.
Provide a way for visitors to download a PDF copy of your resume.

Contact Information
Make it easy for visitors to contact you.
You can:
Add a “Contact” page that sends messages to an email you check regularly.
Or display your email and/or links to your professional platforms.
